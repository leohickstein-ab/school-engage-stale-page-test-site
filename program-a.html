Northern Lakes Aviation Maintenance

Program code: NLA-240.

Credential: Ontario College Diploma.

Campus: Hangar Bay Campus.

Duration: 24 months.

Start terms: September and January.

Admission requirement: Grade 12 English and Grade 11 Mathematics.

Students learn aircraft inspection, maintenance documentation, turbine fundamentals, safety procedures, and maintenance troubleshooting.

Career outcomes include aircraft maintenance technician, hangar maintenance assistant, and aviation quality support coordinator.

Domestic tuition estimate: 4,200 CAD per year.

International tuition estimate: 18,900 CAD per year.
