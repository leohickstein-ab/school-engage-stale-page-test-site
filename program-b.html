Digital Health Records Certificate

Program code: DHR-118.

Credential: Ontario College Certificate.

Campus: Lakeside Health Campus.

Duration: 8 months.

Start terms: May and September.

Admission requirement: Ontario Secondary School Diploma or mature student status.

Students learn clinical data entry, privacy practices, patient record workflows, healthcare terminology, and digital scheduling systems.

Career outcomes include health records clerk, clinic intake assistant, and patient information coordinator.

Domestic tuition estimate: 2,150 CAD.

International tuition estimate: 9,750 CAD.
