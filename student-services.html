Student Services

The Student Success Centre is located in room L210 at Lakeside Campus.

The centre is open Monday to Friday from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM.

Available services include academic advising, accessibility intake, peer tutoring, study skills workshops, and career coaching.

Students can book a tutoring appointment by emailing tutoring@testschool.example.

Accessibility intake appointments require the student number and preferred program name.

The emergency bursary is called the Blue Heron Emergency Bursary and supports urgent transportation or textbook costs.
